During the second term we had the opportunity to complete several personality tests. There was the Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator ( MBTI ) assessment. This test gives insight about yourself and how to interact with other people. It provides a framework for building long term business relationships.

The result of my MBTI - assessment is ESTJ.

Etraversion means that I feel motivated by interaction between other people and gain energy from social interaction.

Sensing means that I am more concrete then abstract. I focus more on details instead of the big picture and I am focused on the present and in a lesser extend on the future.

Thinking means I value objective criteria above personal preference. When making decisions, I generally give more weight to logic then to social considerations.

Judgement means that I prefer to plan my activities and make decisions early.

Enneagram

Enneagram is a model of human personality which is understood and taught as a typology of nine interconnected personality types. According to the test, I am a Motivator.

Reflection

These different assessments helped me to get a better view of myself as a person. It should help me, further in my career, to build up relationships with collegues and business partners.
