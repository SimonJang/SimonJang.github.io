Tribute to Isaac Asimov

One of the "Big Three" science fiction writers

Why a tribute?

I've read the "Foundation" trilogy and decided that Isaac Asimov deserved a tribute page. As one of the "Big Three", Isaac Asimov is considered to be one of the most influential science fiction writers.

This is a FreeCodeCamp assignment.

Biography: overview of his life

  • Born between Oktober 4, 1919 and January 2, 1920 in Petrovichi, Russia
  • Moved to Brooklyn, New York at the age of 3
  • Became a naturalized U.S. citizen in 1928
  • Completed MA in chemistry in 1941
  • Earned a PhD in biochemistry in 1948
  • Started as professor at the Boston University School of Medicine in 1948
  • Turned to full-time writing in 1958
  • Died in 1992
Source: Wikipedia
